Favorite City - Greenville, TX

Image: Downtown Greenville, TX


The city I've chosen to showcase is Greenville, TX. It is known as the birthplace of Audie Murphy, and has a yearly "Audie Days" event that showcases his accomplishements and film history.
Another interesting thing about Greenville, TX is that there used to be a minor league baseball team there called the Greenville Majors. The Greenville Majors actually beat the New York Yankees in a game in 1949.
